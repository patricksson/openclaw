Why WhatsApp Specifically?

You might wonder why WhatsApp rather than SMS, email, or a website chatbot. The answer is in one number.

WhatsApp messages have a 98% open rate. Email sits at roughly 20%. That is according to WhatsApp's own business blog.

Think about what that means in practice. If you send 100 appointment reminders via email, about 20 people will open them. Send those same 100 reminders via WhatsApp, and 98 people will see them.

That is not a marginal improvement. That is a completely different category of communication. And it explains why the businesses in those case studies saw the results they did. Their messages were actually being read.

There is a practical reason too. WhatsApp is already on your customers' phones. They use it every day. They do not need to download a new app, create a new account, or change any behavior. They just message you the same way they message their friends.

For small businesses, that matters enormously. The less friction between "customer has a question" and "customer gets an answer," the more customers you keep.
