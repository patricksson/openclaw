How to Use This Dashboard

A quick guide to getting the most out of your AI receptionist.

1

Setup — Connect WhatsApp

Open WhatsApp Business on your phone, go to Settings > Linked Devices > Link a Device, then scan the QR code shown on the Setup tab. If you're on mobile, switch to the "Phone Number" method and enter your number to get a pairing code instead. Your AI receptionist goes live immediately. You only need to pair once — it stays connected even if you close this dashboard.

2

Pause & Disconnect — Control your agent

Once connected, you can toggle the agent on or off using the switch on the Setup tab. Toggling off pauses AI responses without disconnecting WhatsApp, so you can take over manually. To fully disconnect, use the "Disconnect WhatsApp" button. This unlinks the device entirely and you will need to scan or pair again to reconnect.

3

Agent Settings — Tell the AI about your business

Fill in your services, pricing, business hours, and policies. The more detail you provide, the better the AI answers customer questions. Changes apply instantly — no need to restart anything. Think of it as writing a cheat sheet for your best employee.

4

Bookings — Set your availability

Enable the days you work, set your hours, and add your appointment types (e.g. "Consultation — 30 min"). The AI will only offer times that are genuinely free. If you also take bookings by phone, connect Google Calendar so everything stays in sync.

5

Leads — See who's contacted you

Every customer who messages your WhatsApp gets saved as a lead automatically. You can update their status (New, Contacted, Qualified, Won, Lost), add notes, and export the list as a CSV. Use the search and filters to find specific customers quickly.

6

Usage & Billing — Track conversations and upgrade

The Usage tab shows how many conversations your AI has handled this month and how close you are to your plan limit. When you need more capacity, head to Billing to upgrade to Pro (150 conversations) or Max (unlimited).

Tips for getting the best results

Be specific in your settings

Instead of "We do plumbing," write "Emergency callouts, boiler repairs, bathroom installations, tap replacements." The AI matches customer requests to your exact services.

Include pricing where possible

Customers ask about cost more than anything else. Even a range ("Boiler service: £80-120") is better than nothing.

Update your hours if they change

The AI tells customers when you're available. If your hours change seasonally, update them so customers get accurate info.

Check your leads regularly

The AI captures contact details, but following up is on you. Customers who get a quick callback convert at a much higher rate.

Don't use WhatsApp for cold outreach

The AI is designed as a receptionist. Sending unsolicited marketing messages can get your WhatsApp number flagged and reported to Meta.
