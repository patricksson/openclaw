Frequently Asked Questions

How does the setup process work?

Sign up with your email, enter your business details (services, hours, prices), and scan a QR code to connect WhatsApp. Your AI receptionist is live in about 10 minutes. No coding or technical skills needed.

Do I need any technical skills?

No. Everything runs in the cloud. You just enter your business info and scan a QR code with WhatsApp. No server, no coding, no technical setup required.

What if something breaks after setup?

Email us at support@automatyn.co. Starter and Pro plans include priority support. You can also update your bot's info anytime from the dashboard.

What's your refund policy?

Paid plans come with a 7-day money-back guarantee. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. The free plan has no commitment at all.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

What counts as a "conversation"?

A conversation = one customer messaging you within a 24-hour window, regardless of how many messages they send back and forth. This matches Meta's own WhatsApp Business billing definition. If the same customer messages you 10 times in a day, that's still one conversation, not ten.

What channels do you support?

Currently WhatsApp is the primary channel. We're adding Telegram, Instagram DMs, and more soon. Prefer to self-host on all channels? Check out OpenClaw (open source).
