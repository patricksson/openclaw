Frequently Asked Questions

How does the setup process work?

After a free consultation call, we'll gather your requirements and configure OpenClaw for your needs. You'll receive setup files to run on your server, or we can do a screen-share session. Most setups complete within 24-48 hours.

Do I need to give you access to my server?

No. We generate custom setup scripts that you run on your own machine. Your API keys and data never leave your server. For Enterprise clients, we can optionally do a screen-share session.

What if something breaks after setup?

All plans include email support. Monthly support subscribers get priority response within 4 hours. We troubleshoot issues and provide fixes quickly.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If we can't get your OpenClaw setup working, you get a full refund. No questions asked.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade by paying the difference between your current plan and the new one. Contact us for upgrade pricing.

What channels do you support?

Telegram, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp, Signal, iMessage, and more. If OpenClaw supports it, we can configure it.
