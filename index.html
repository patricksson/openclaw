Frequently Asked Questions

            


            

                

                    How long does setup take?
                    
Most setups complete within 24-48 hours after payment. Complex enterprise setups may take 3-5 business days.

                

                

                    Do I need to give you access to my server?
                    
No. We generate a custom setup script that you run on your machine. Your tokens and data never leave your server. For hands-on setup, we can arrange a screen-share session.

                

                

                    What if something breaks?
                    
All plans include email support. Monthly support subscribers get priority response and proactive monitoring. We'll troubleshoot and fix issues remotely.

                

                

                    Can I upgrade my plan later?
                    
Yes. You can upgrade from Foundation to Professional or Enterprise by paying the difference. Contact us for upgrade pricing.

                

                

                    Do you offer refunds?
                    
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if we cannot get your OpenClaw setup working. We've never had a setup we couldn't complete.

                

                

                    What's included in monthly support?
                    
Monthly support includes: OpenClaw updates, security patches, troubleshooting (up to 4 hours/month), configuration adjustments, and priority email response within 4 hours.
