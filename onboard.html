Important: how to use safely

Automatyn connects via WhatsApp Web (same as using WhatsApp on a browser). To keep your number safe:

  • ✓Use it to reply to customer messages — this is what it's built for
  • ✓Use a business-dedicated number (not your personal WhatsApp)
  • ✗Don't send bulk/cold messages to people who haven't messaged you first
  • ✗Don't use it for marketing broadcasts to strangers

WhatsApp can restrict numbers that violate their terms. Used properly (reactive customer replies), the risk is low. Used improperly (cold outreach/spam), your number can be banned. You are responsible for how you use it.
