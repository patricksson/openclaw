Questions plumbers ask

Straight answers, no sales spin.

Will it quote the right price and mention my credentials?

Yes. During setup you enter your credentials (Gas Safe number if you have one), callout fee, hourly rate, and what jobs you cover. The AI only quotes what you tell it to quote. It'll never invent a price.

What happens if the AI gets something wrong?

You can take over any chat from the dashboard with one toggle. The AI stops replying on that thread and you handle it yourself. Flip it back on when you're done. The customer never sees any handover.

Do I need a new phone number?

No. You connect your existing WhatsApp number (Business or personal). Setup is the same QR-scan as linking WhatsApp Web — your phone stays logged in.

Will customers know it's a bot?

On the free Starter tier, there's a small "AI agent by Automatyn" footer on replies. Pro and Max remove it entirely. Either way the tone is set to plumber-natural: "right, sounds like the filling loop", not corporate speak.

What if I can't do a job the AI booked?

Connect Google Calendar (Pro plan). The AI only offers slots that are genuinely free. If you want it to never auto-book, set it to "qualify and hand over" mode — it gathers the job details and tells the customer you'll confirm timing.

Is it safe? Where does the data go?

UK-based, GDPR-compliant. Your business info is stored on ISO 27001-certified infrastructure. Customer messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude API under their enterprise data policy — no training on your data. You can export or delete everything anytime.

What counts as a "conversation"?

One customer chatting with you within a 24-hour window, no matter how many back-and-forth messages. Same definition Meta uses for WhatsApp Business billing. If a customer messages you 10 times in a day, that's one conversation.

Can I cancel?

One click from your dashboard. 7-day money-back on paid plans. Free tier has no commitment.
