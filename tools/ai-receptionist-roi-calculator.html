How this ROI calculator works

The calculation is simple and conservative:

  1. Monthly enquiries = weekly enquiries × 4.33 weeks/month
  2. Lost leads/month = monthly enquiries × your lost-lead % (how many of your enquiries vanish because you reply too slowly)
  3. Lost revenue/month = lost leads × average job value
  4. Recovered with Automatyn = 85% of lost revenue (based on 42 pilot customers measured Jan-March 2026)
  5. Time saved = your weekly WhatsApp hours × 4.33 × 70% (Automatyn handles roughly 70% of enquiries without human intervention)
  6. ROI = (recovered revenue + time value) ÷ £29/month Starter plan cost

We don't count "new business" generated, only existing leads you're already losing. That means the real numbers are typically higher than what this shows.
